# RoomLight — The Smart Hotel Room Light Controller

### Design Once, Sync Everywhere!


LAB Lab announced today a new hotel room lighting controller — RoomLight — a centralized system that allows hotels to design their lighting experience once and instantly syncit across every room.

Every hotel knows the challenge: inconsistent lighting scenes, time-consuming room setup, and costly
manual adjustments. RoomLight eliminates the hassle. Create the perfect lighting design once and deploy it to every room automatically.

> "We wanted to give hotels a smarter way to deliver a consistent guest experience while reducing operational complexity," said a Product Manager at LAB Lab.

RoomLight allows hotel operators to configure scenes for arrival, relaxation, work, and sleep from a central dashboard. Adjust brightness, color temperature, and mood settings — then synchronize them across one floor, one building, or the entire property in seconds.

Need to update the ambiance? Simply modify the master design. Every connected room updates automatically — no technician visits, no manual reprogramming, no disruption to guests.
